The Ceremony

The ceremony will be outside beside the lake in the Boston Public Garden. It will not last more than 20 minutes. There will be a small number of seats available for those who need them, but we hope most of you will stand the whole time (like us!).

The dress code for the wedding day is semiformal (men in suits, women in cocktail attire). We'll be pretty formal with our white dress and morning suit, so you should feel free to get fancy.

After the ceremony, we will all walk the 1/4 mile to the reception.

The Reception

The reception is at the College Club of Boston, a quaint townhouse on Commonwealth Avenue 1/4 mile from the ceremony location.

Dinner and drinks provided. Bring your own dancing shoes.

About Us

We met nine years ago at the College of William & Mary. After briefly meeting during freshman orientation, we ended up sitting next to each other on our first day of choir. We started hanging out after choir, and on our first date we jumped over the Governor’s Palace wall in Colonial Williamsburg (a student tradition of nebulous legality).

Over the last nine years, we’ve been through good and bad together. But after going through breakups/reunions, long distance/sharing an apartment, we decided that life is more fun together. We cannot wait to share the rest of our lives with each other.

After much deliberation and consideration of our lives together, we have decided to change both of our names. We want a name that we can share as a couple and later with our children as a family. However, we could not decide whose name to use. In the end, we decided to merge our last names into something new, signifying our union while still honoring our roots. After October 9th, we will be Cory and Melanie Chainsman.
